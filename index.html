Downtown Vancouver

Economic Development

Downtown Vancouver has seen a lot of changes in recent years. There are new building projects in all parts of the area. Hundreds of apartments have been added. The economy in downtown Vancouver is growing. This website is a summary of the city of Vancouver’s economic development plan for downtown.

The economic development website is a great source of information about future of Vancouver. Among many useful tools about doing business in the city, there is a large section about the economic development of downtown Vancouver. There is information about making downtown more of a destination for tourist, the potential economic impact of food carts, the large waterfront redevelopment, and developing the currently empty block 10
