Mobile Food Vending

There are many opportunities for mobile food vending in Downtown Vancouver. Mobile food vending or food carts offer a quick and easy way to get a meal. There are many different varieties of food served. Mobile food vendors offer a unique and exciting experience. Food carts are a niche and develop a community of local like minded people.

Customers of mobile food vending locations also bring business to other restaurants and businesses in in the area. In order to operate food trucks, are required to obtain a mobile food vending permit and a street use permit. The City of Vancouver offer food vending opportunities for special events. Food carts can offer a path to community development and can help in increasing infrastructure.
