Destination Downtown

The City of Vancouver has been a destination for local Vancouver residents. In past ten year the downtown area has been going through development increases. With the numerous restaurants, stores and breweries bringing customers into the downtown area. Destination Downtown is the cooperation of the City of Vancouver and C-TRAN, to work toward attracting visitors, employees and visitors.

The main focus is to reduce drive time and street parking, in order to make downtown more dynamic and successful. The City of Vancouver offers a program called MyDestinationDowntown.com. This program allows employees of downtown to register and track their commute. By suggesting new options forms of transportation, such as biking, transit, walking, and carpool. MyDestinationDowntown.com offers the free services: customized trip planning, trial permits, and maps of downtown.
